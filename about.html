About First Class Softee

Bringing joy, one scoop at a time — right here in Manchester, NH.

Who We Are

First Class Softee is Manchester, New Hampshire's go-to mobile soft serve ice cream experience. We're a locally owned, community-focused business that loves bringing smiles to people of all ages — whether it's a sunny afternoon at a Hooksett town fair, a neighborhood block party in Bedford, or a backyard birthday in Goffstown.

From classic vanilla twists to wild specialty cones loaded with toppings, we craft every order fresh right from our truck. Our menu features over 40 soft serve creations — sundaes, waffle cones, double cones, specialty Merlins, shakes, and more — so there's truly something for everyone, from kids to grandparents.

We started First Class Softee because we believe nothing brings people together quite like ice cream. There's something special about hearing that ice cream truck music, lining up at the window, and watching the smiles as everyone picks their favorite treat. That moment of joy is what drives us every single day.

We're proud to be part of the greater Manchester community — from Londonderry to Auburn to Merrimack — and we love showing up at the events that matter most to you. If it's happening in the area, chances are we want to be there!
