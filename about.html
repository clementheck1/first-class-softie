About First Class Softee

Bringing joy, one scoop at a time — right here in Manchester, NH.

Who We Are

First Class Softee is Manchester, New Hampshire's go-to mobile soft serve ice cream experience. We're a local, community-focused business that loves bringing smiles to people of all ages — whether it's a sunny afternoon at a town fair or a neighborhood block party.

From classic vanilla twists to wild specialty cones loaded with toppings, we craft every order fresh right from our truck. Our menu is packed with options — sundaes, waffle cones, double cones, shakes, and more — so there's truly something for everyone.

We're proud to be part of the Manchester community and we love showing up at the events that matter most to you. If it's happening in the area, chances are we want to be there!
